Our Philosophy

At Archetypes & Sol, our philosophy is inspired by the Jungian archetypes of the Explorer and Sage. We understand that giving all your energy to others makes it hard to focus on your own promotion, strategy and marketing, which is where we come in. We help you identify and align with the fundamentals of what makes you different, what drives you, such as your innate calling. We'll ensure your message resonates on a profound level. Whilst we are inspired by Jungian archetypes, we are not tied to this, and tailor our approach to each individual client's needs.

Our Way of Working

Brief introduction to your way of working...

Being Your Holistic Champion

While we aren't business coaches, our holistic approach offers more than just transactional services— we adapt to your specific needs, guiding you through the process of promoting your practice with confidence.

Unearthing Your Treasures

Drawing on the framework of Jungian archetypes, we will define your unique story and use this as a starting point. Whether you embody the Healer, the Sage, or the Explorer or more, we help you articulate these elements in a way that speaks directly to those who need your services. Of course we are not tied to any form of thinking or therapy or way of thinking - so if this framework is not your vibe we can adapt to what works for you.

Sensitive and Ego-Free Approach

At Archetypes & Sol, we believe in no hard selling or pushing you into anything that doesn't feel right or true to you. First and foremost, we're here to listen and allow you to be vulnerable if you so wish, creating a safe space to do so. We simply help shine a light on any areas where you feel stuck in your business or projects, much like you would do for your own clients.

Tough Love

There is a time and place for delving into the shadows, and the need for honest feedback about your profile. It can however be hard when it is about your own practice and how you communicate. We never dictate what you should do or force you to change your approach. Instead, we guide you in stripping out jargon and humanising your copy, so it truly connects with your audience.
